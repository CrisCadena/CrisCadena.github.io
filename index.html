What is the game about?

The game is a MMO (massive multiplayer online game) and is very fun to play with friends. There is a great variety of items and skills that make your champion powerful. Each skill is for the most part unique to every other champion. Although, you can see some similarities.

The reason why I like to play this game is because all the possible combinations that you could do in order to conquer the enemy's base. There are four different roles that you could play as (top laner, jungle, mid, attack damage and support) and each champion has a specific afinity to one of this roles. However, some champions could be played in different lanes. For example, you could be a magical assassin, a healer or a sniper. It all depends on the synergy and the mastery level that you have for that champion. The game can be toxic when playing alone but you can always mute the toxic ones so you won't receive any negative vibes from them.

E-sports

The game celebrates a championship for the professional gamers from all over the globe. You could watch the matches according to your region or any region.

Reasons for my 3 champions

Teemo is a deadly yordle that uses poison as damage. This champion can set up mines (mushrooms) all over the map. When touched, these mines can do some deadly damage to any enemy that step in. When built the right items and as long as the enemy team doesn't build magic resist, you could kill them in just a few seconds when they touch one of the mines. There's a lot of hatred against this champion in the LOL community but I like him.

Malzahar is a magician that was created from the void. His spells make enemies silenced, making them unable to perfom any abilities and skills. They can also be surpressed making them being able to do nothing, not even move. I consider this champion easy to use and very beneficial to the team synergy.

Soraka is a healer that can also silence enemies. Her majestic ultimate call the stars and immediately heals everyone in your team no matter where you are in the map. This skill can be very helpful because it can make a teammate win a one on one fight that they were about to lose for so little. Keeping everyone at full health is something I enjoy doing it whenever I play this champion.
